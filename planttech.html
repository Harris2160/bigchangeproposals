Meeting 1: Discovery

Escaping the "Admin After-Hours" Trap

Attendees: Lucy (Plant-Tech Ltd), Harris Tariq (BigChange), Matthew Beecham (BigChange).

Plant-Tech is at a pivotal growth point. Currently a one-man band (Dan) with Lucy managing the back-office, you are ready to add a second engineer. Key pain points identified:

  • Manual Typing Hell: Dan spends his evenings typing up hand-written LOLER notes into digital forms. This takes hours of his personal time.
  • Google Calendar Bottleneck: Managing schedules on a standard calendar is "messy" and doesn't allow for real-time asset or map views.
  • LOLA Compliance: You need your exact templates to be digitised so they are identical to current reports but populated instantly on-site.
  • Scale Paralysis: You have the van ready for a new engineer but lack the system to track their productivity and location reliably.

Current State vs. Future State

Transitioning from manual notebooks to an automated, map-based enterprise workflow.

Current State (Manual & Fragmented)

  • Dual Admin: Dan takes notes on-site, then Lucy or Dan re-types them at home. Double the work.
  • Scheduling Blindness: Google Calendar shows the 'what' but not the 'where' or 'who is nearest'.
  • Xero Manual Entry: Invoices have to be manually keyed from notes into Xero, leading to errors.
  • Asset Tracking: No central log of machine LOLER history, requiring manual checks for due dates.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Zero Re-typing: Dan completes the digital LOLER on a rugged tablet. The professional PDF is ready instantly.
  • Map-Based Intelligent Scheduling: See every machine, job, and van on a live map for optimized routing.
  • Platinum Xero Integration: Raise an invoice in BigChange and it appears in your Xero ledger automatically.
  • Automatic Compliance: System pings Lucy and the customer when a LOLER is due in 30 days.
